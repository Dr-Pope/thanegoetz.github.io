2026.04.11 CHESS on Broadway [PERSONAL]

Caught the Broadway revival of CHESS tonight — my first Broadway show, ever. A close friend invited me and I figured what the heck, why not. By the end of the opening act I understood the allure. Difficult and Dangerous Times in particular was wonderful.

The trip up was worth every mile and every train transfer. Left the theatre with "One Night in Bangkok" on permanent loop in my head.

More thoughts once I've slept on it.
