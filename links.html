Here are all the sites I think are worth visiting!! I've tried to organize them by category. If I link to you, please link back — that's just good web etiquette!! 🌐
⚠ NOTE: All links were working as of March 2026. The web changes fast — if something is broken please sign the guestbook and let me know!!
🔭 ASTRONOMY & SPACE
  • NASA Official Homepage The big one. Everything space, from NASA.
  • Hubble Space Telescope Gallery Beautiful deep field images, free to download!!
  • Sky & Telescope Magazine Best astronomy magazine online. Subscribe!!!
  • ADS Abstract Service Find peer-reviewed astrophysics papers for free.
  • NASA/IPAC Extragalactic Database (NED) Amazing database for AGN and galaxy research.
  • The Messier Catalog Pages Every Messier object with full screen skymap. Essential!!
  • Bad Astronomy (Phil Plait) Debunking space myths and pseudoscience. Funny too!!
  • SETI Institute Searching for extraterrestrial intelligence since 1984.
⚛ PHYSICS & SCIENCE
  • OpenStax Great free resource for free textbooks in various subjects.
  • HyperPhysics (Georgia State) Incredible hyperlinked physics reference. I use this constantly.
  • Eric Weisstein's World of Physics Like an encyclopedia but for physics. Very thorough.
  • arXiv.org Preprints Free preprint papers — astro-ph section is great.
  • Carl Sagan Portal Official site celebrating one of the greatest science communicators ever.
💾 SOFTWARE & TOOLS
  • SAOImage DS9 The FITS viewer I use for all my research. Free!!
  • DeepSkyStacker Free stacking software for astrophotography.
  • Astropy Project Python library for astronomy. Essential for research!!
  • Python.org The best scripting language for science and everything else.
  • Stellarium Free open-source planetarium for planning observations.
🎲 D&D & GAMING
  • Wizards of the Coast — D&D Official D&D site. All the official rules and supplements.
  • The Hypertext d20 SRD All the 3.5e rules online for free. Super useful at the table.
  • RPG.net Forums Best place to discuss tabletop RPG stuff online.
  • Space Station 14 Open source rebuild of SS13. The chaos game, now with source code!! NEW
