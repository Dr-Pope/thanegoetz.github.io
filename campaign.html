// DOSSIER BRIEFING — HOW TO FILL THIS OUT //

▸ THE BASICS

Standard D&D 5e rules apply for ability scores, proficiency, and most skills. Enter your ability scores (STR, DEX, CON, INT, WIS, CHA) and modifiers auto-calculate. Enter your level and proficiency bonus updates automatically (+2 @ lvls 1–4, +3 @ 5–8, +4 @ 9–12, +5 @ 13–16, +6 @ 17–20).

▸ RESKINNED SKILLS

Some classic skills have been flavor-swapped for the setting. They use the same ability modifier and DC table as their 5e counterpart — only the name changes.
  • Tech / Hacking (INT) — Arcana reskin: computers, networks, ICE
  • Sciences (INT) — Nature reskin: xenobiology, physics, chemistry
  • Corp Law (INT) — Religion reskin: contract law, corporate charter, bylaws
  • Drone Handling (WIS) — Animal Handling reskin: remote rigs & drones

▸ AUGMENTATIONS & CYBERNETICS

Log each implant's location (arm / eye / spine / neural), its name, and the mechanical effect. Cybernetics count against your Biometric Override meter (bottom of page 3). At 100% biometric you roll on the Rogue AI Table.

▸ CORP STANDING & FACTIONS

Each corp has two sliders: OPINION OF YOU (personal rep) and PUBLIC OPINION (how the public views them). Range is −100 to +100. Below −50 with any corp and bounty hunters become a concern. Above +50 and you get contract offers.
